# Yandex Games Metadata (EN)

**Title**: Hexi

**SEO Description** (<=160):
A hex‑grid puzzle where you guide a turtle, capture colored tiles, and build color combos for high scores.

**About the Game** (<=1000):
Hexi is a minimalist puzzle on a hexagonal grid. Control the turtle, capture colored tiles, and move them to empty cells to form strong color combinations. Capture success depends on how close the tile color is to the base orange. It’s a calm, thoughtful game focused on planning and precision.

**How to Play** (<=1000):
Desktop: move with arrow keys. Hold Space to charge capture on a colored tile, move to an empty spot, and release Space to place it.
Mobile: tap cells to move. Tap ACT to attempt capture and tap ACT again to place the tile on an empty spot.
